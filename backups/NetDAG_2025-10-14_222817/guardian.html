Intelligence at the Core: The NetDAG Guardian

The Guardian is our AI stability layer. It predicts liquidity needs, detects anomalies, and proposes curve optimizations — with transparent governance approval.

What is the NetDAG Guardian?

The NetDAG Guardian is an AI stability layer that continuously analyzes on-chain activity—transaction flows, buy/sell pressure, dApp usage, node performance—and proposes transparent, governance-approved adjustments to keep the economy healthy.

  • Anomaly detection: flags suspicious trading and spam patterns.
  • Liquidity forecasting: anticipates demand for reserves.
  • Curve proposals: suggests slope/reserve updates to smooth volatility.
  • Fair rewards: encourages real participation, not wash activity.

How It Works: A Continuous Feedback Loop

1) Data Capture

Streams DAG events: volume, node uptime/latency, dApp engagement, Provenance scans, and dVPN sessions.

2) Neural Analysis

Models forecast stress and opportunity and generate recommended parameters (e.g., slope, reserve ratio).

3) Smart Feedback

Recommendations go on-chain via multisig/DAO. Approved updates adjust the bonding curve or policy modules.

The Guardian in Action

Example: a partner verifies 10,000 items via Provenance. The Guardian forecasts higher liquidity demand, proposes a small slope increase so reserves grow faster, and publishes the event to a public dashboard—stability without drama.
