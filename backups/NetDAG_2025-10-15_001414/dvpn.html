What is a Decentralized VPN (dVPN)?

A dVPN replaces centralized servers with a global mesh of independent nodes. Node operators earn NDG for sharing bandwidth; users connect through encrypted multi-hop routes. Payments happen automatically on-chain — no logs, no gatekeepers.

How It Works — 3 Steps

1) Launch

Open the dVPN app to fetch available, high-reputation nodes from the DAG network.

2) Connect

The NetDAG Guardian selects the fastest, most trustworthy multi-hop route.

3) Browse

Pay-as-you-go in NDG per minute or GB. A share goes to nodes; the rest strengthens the reserve & charity pool.
