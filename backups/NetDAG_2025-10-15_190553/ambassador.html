Join the Core: Become a NetDAG Ambassador

Help build a more stable, trustworthy, and useful Web3 — educate, create, and grow the community.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Passionate about Web3: You believe in real utility and user protection.
  • Community leaders: You engage on Telegram, Discord, and X.
  • Content creators: Writers, video creators, designers, translators.
  • Networkers: Connections in crypto or industries like logistics, finance, privacy.

What You’ll Do

Educate the Community

Answer questions, host discussions, and help newcomers get started.

Create Content

Write posts, make videos, or design graphics that explain NetDAG simply.

Spread the Word

Share announcements and represent NetDAG in relevant communities.

Provide Feedback

Be a bridge to the core team — share insights and product suggestions.

What You’ll Receive

  • Exclusive Access: Direct line to the core team and roadmap insights.
  • NDG Rewards: Allocation from the Ecosystem Incentives fund, based on contribution.
  • Recognition: Official role on community platforms + website feature.
  • Support & Resources: Branded materials and coordination help.

How to Apply

Send an email to ambassadors@netdag.com with:

  1. A brief introduction about yourself.
  2. Why you’re passionate about NetDAG.
  3. Links to your social profiles (X, Telegram, etc.).
  4. How you plan to contribute to the ecosystem.
