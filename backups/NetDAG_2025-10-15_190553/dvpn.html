    What is a dVPN?

    A dVPN replaces centralized servers with a global peer-to-peer network of nodes. Node owners share bandwidth and earn NDG tokens while users connect through encrypted, multi-hop routes — without logs or a single point of failure.

    How It Works: 3 Steps

    1) Launch

    Open the app to fetch a list of reputable nodes from the DAG network.

    2) One-Click Connect

    The NetDAG Guardian auto-selects the fastest, most trustworthy route.

    3) Browse

    Pay-as-you-go in NDG per minute or per GB. A portion rewards node providers; the rest strengthens reserves and charity.
