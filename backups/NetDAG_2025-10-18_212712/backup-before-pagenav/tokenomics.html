    Tokenomics

    Predictable liquidity. Transparent allocations. Long-term alignment.

    Overview

    NDG uses a bonding curve to convert network demand into a growing on-chain reserve. Buys mint tokens and strengthen the reserve; sells burn supply and release from that reserve. The result is native liquidity and smooth, math-driven pricing.

    Supply & Sale

    Maximum Supply

    1,000,000,000 NDG (hard cap).

    Supply is enforced by contracts; no hidden mint.

    Presale

    Starts at $0.03 with planned step-ups.

    Early buy-only phase may apply to fill the reserve and set a price floor.

    Stability Mechanics

    Bonding Curve

    Price follows supply; liquidity is embedded. This reduces flash-crash risk and removes order-book dependence.

    Guardian Oversight

    The Guardian AI analyzes telemetry and proposes parameter updates (slope, reserve ratio) for DAO approval.

    Presale Safeguards

    Buy-only windows, vesting on large sells, and circuit-breaker options for orderly markets.

    Charity Flow

    A protocol share (e.g., 2%) is directed on-chain to a charity pool with auditable disbursements.
