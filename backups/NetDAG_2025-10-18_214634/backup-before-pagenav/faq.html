    Frequently Asked Questions

    The most important answers—clear and transparent.

    What is NetDAG? +
    NetDAG is a trust-first Web3 stack combining a high-throughput DAG ledger, a stabilizing bonding curve, and the Guardian AI for predictive optimization. It enables real-world apps like Provenance and dVPN.
    How does the bonding curve protect price? +
    The token price is tied to supply by a function. Buys mint and add to a reserve; sells burn and release from it. Instead of collapse, price moves smoothly along the curve—reducing volatility and ensuring liquidity.
    What does the Guardian AI do? +
    It monitors network telemetry, forecasts liquidity needs, detects anomalies, and proposes parameter updates. All significant changes are subject to multisig/DAO approval—automation with oversight.
    Is NetDAG eco-friendly? +
    Yes. The DAG architecture minimizes redundant work and supports high throughput at low energy cost, making micropayments and global usage sustainable.
    What is the token supply and presale price? +
    Max supply 1,000,000,000 NDG. Presale starts at $0.03 with scheduled increases, plus safeguards such as buy-only phases and vesting for large sells.
    How does charity funding work? +
    A portion of protocol revenue (e.g., 2%) flows automatically into an on-chain charity pool. Disbursements are transparent and community-auditable.
    Where can I follow updates? +
    Our website blog and social channels share releases, proposals, audits, and roadmap progress.
