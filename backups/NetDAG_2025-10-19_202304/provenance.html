What is NetDAG Provenance?

NetDAG Provenance is a proof-of-origin system. When a buyer scans a product’s QR tag, they see immutable data from the blockchain — where it was made, when it shipped, and who certified it — not a marketing page.

How It Works: The Journey to Verifiable Trust

1) Registration

Verified producers upload batch details and documents to the Provenance Portal.

2) Blockchain Record

A smart contract secures the data on the DAG ledger — time, location, signer — immutably.

3) Instant Verification

Consumers scan the product’s QR code to retrieve the proof from the network.

4) Value Loop

Each scan triggers a tiny NDG transaction, strengthening the bonding-curve reserve and the charity fund.

Why It Matters: Benefits Across the Supply Chain

  • For Businesses: “Authenticity as a Service” for food, fashion, pharma, and more.
  • For Consumers: Verify origin instantly — no app, no third-party trust.
  • For the Ecosystem: Each scan strengthens NDG’s liquidity engine via the bonding curve.
  • For Social Impact: A percentage of verification fees flows to the charity reserve.
