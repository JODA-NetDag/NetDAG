What is a Decentralized VPN (dVPN)?

A dVPN replaces a single, vulnerable company server with a global, peer-to-peer network of independent nodes. Node operators share bandwidth and earn NDG; users connect through encrypted, multi-hop routes with pricing and rewards handled by smart contracts — no central logs, no single point of failure.

  • Community-run nodes: Earn NDG by contributing bandwidth and reliability.
  • Multi-hop privacy: Traffic is routed across independent nodes to reduce correlation risk.
  • On-chain payments: Usage is settled automatically and transparently.

How It Works: Secure Browsing in 3 Steps

1) Launch

The app fetches a list of high-reputation nodes from the DAG network.

2) One-Click Connect

The NetDAG Guardian selects a fast, trustworthy route across multiple nodes.

3) Pay-as-you-go

Pay per minute or per-GB in NDG; rewards flow to node operators and the ecosystem reserve.
