    Growth with a Purpose

    NetDAG’s Charity Initiative turns everyday network activity into continuous real-world impact.

    The Charity-First Model

    Technology’s success should be measured by the good it enables. NetDAG hard-codes philanthropy into the protocol so impact scales with adoption. A portion of activity is streamed to an on-chain pool that the community can trace, audit, and govern transparently.

    How It Works: The 2% Contribution

    Continuous Flow

    From eligible revenue or protocol fees, 2% is automatically routed to the Charity Pool. This is not a one-off donation; it is a sustainable, programmatic stream.

    Protocol-Level Logic

    Contributions occur at the smart-contract layer. Transactions like dVPN usage or Provenance verifications participate according to governance-approved parameters.

    Transparency and Trust

    • On-Chain Accounting: Inflows/outflows are recorded on the ledger for anyone to verify.
    • Open Disbursements: Grants to verified charities are published with tx hashes and reports.
    • Community Governance: The DAO sets eligibility, partner lists, and review cadence.
    • Public Reporting: Dashboards visualize totals, recipients, and impact notes.

    Get Involved

    NetDAG welcomes responsible NGOs, local initiatives, and global partners aligned with transparent, measurable impact. If your organization is interested in collaboration, please reach out through our contact page. Community proposals for new recipients can be submitted to the DAO.
