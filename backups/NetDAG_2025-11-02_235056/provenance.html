Trust Written in Code: NetDAG Provenance

NetDAG Provenance is a proof-of-origin system. When a buyer scans a product’s QR tag, they see immutable data from the blockchain — where it was made, when it shipped, and who certified it — not a marketing page.

The End of Counterfeiting. The Dawn of Real Blockchain Utility.

Counterfeiting drains hundreds of billions annually and erodes consumer trust. Legacy “auth” labels are paper-thin and easy to fake. NetDAG Provenance replaces doubt with cryptographic proof anyone can verify in seconds.

What changes now: authenticity becomes a public good. A scan reveals tamper-evident batch data, certifiers, timestamps, and movement—anchored to smart contracts, not a brand’s private database.

Simple flow: Scan → Verify → Confirm. The consumer gets a clear, on-chain confirmation badge and a durable receipt of authenticity.

Built for Everyday Use — at Global Scale

We bring smart-contract certainty into daily commerce. Pharmaceutical lots, luxury goods, electronics, and fashion can all be registered, certified, and verified via one unified protocol that scales with demand.

  • Public Verifiability: Anyone can scan, anywhere. No accounts. No permissions.
  • Producer + Certifier Flow: Producers publish batch metadata; approved certifiers co-sign where required.
  • Audit Trail: Every change is on-chain—time, place, signers—so the record outlives the label.

How It Works: The Journey to Verifiable Trust

Step Action What the User Gets
1) Registration Verified producers upload batch details and documents. Immutable batch handle and QR label.
2) Blockchain Record Smart contract secures hash + metadata; certifiers co-sign where required. Trusted, publicly verifiable record.
3) Instant Verification Consumer scans QR; data loads from chain. On-chain authenticity receipt in seconds.
4) Value Loop Each scan triggers a tiny NDG fee back to the ecosystem. Rewards honest producers and network validators.
