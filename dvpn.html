    Why NetDAG is Investing in dVPN

    NetDAG’s decision to align and invest heavily in Decentralized VPN (dVPN) technology is a strategic move that embodies our core values and positions us at the forefront of the Web3 infrastructure market. We strongly encourage partners to join this high-growth sector.

    1) Core Value Alignment: Censorship Resistance & Trustlessness

    Traditional VPNs create a single point of failure. They can be pressured by regulators or compromised by attackers. A dVPN removes the single point of trust by routing traffic through an independent, peer-to-peer mesh so no single entity—corporate or governmental—can access, censor, or shut it down. This aligns with the immutability and neutrality of blockchain.

    2) Building Web3 Infrastructure: DePIN Market Leadership

    • Incentivized Scalability: NDG rewards allow anyone to contribute unused bandwidth as exit nodes, scaling the network rapidly without centralized server farms.
    • Security for Crypto Users: dVPN adds a vital privacy layer for traders, builders, and communities—safely accessing global exchanges and dApps across borders.

    3) Future-Proofing Against Centralized Regulation

    dVPNs are non-custodial and jurisdiction-agnostic, making them resilient to regulatory capture that plagues centralized providers. This ensures long-term availability while upholding user sovereignty.

    How It Works

    Launch

    The app sources high-reputation nodes from the NetDAG network for a fast, reliable start.

    Connect

    The Guardian scores routes and selects a private, multi-hop path across independent operators.

    Pay-as-You-Go

    Usage is settled in NDG (per minute or per GiB). Rewards flow automatically to node operators.
