What is the NetDAG Bonding Curve?

The Bonding Curve is a mathematical pricing model where the token price is a function of circulating supply. Buys mint along the curve and lock reserve backing; sells burn along the same curve, creating continuous, predictable liquidity. This reduces sudden crashes caused by order-book panic. 

P(S) = a·S + b

Mint cost equals the area under the curve (integral of P). A portion is locked as backing, making NDG asset-backed from genesis.

What is the NetDAG Guardian (AI layer)?

The Guardian is an AI stability layer that analyzes on-chain activity (volume, buy/sell pressure, node health, dApp usage). It proposes adjustments (e.g., slope, reserve ratio, fee splits) to keep the system stable. Proposals require on-chain multisig/DAO approval — the AI advises, the community decides.

How does the decentralized VPN (dVPN) work?

A peer-to-peer network of community nodes provides bandwidth. Users pay per minute/GB in NDG; node operators earn NDG. The Guardian optimizes routing, detects malicious nodes, and helps keep pricing predictable through the bonding-curve model.

What is NetDAG Provenance?

A proof-of-origin system: producers register batches, logistics events are recorded on-chain, and consumers verify with a simple QR scan. Each scan strengthens liquidity (via tiny NDG flow) and contributes 2% to charity.
